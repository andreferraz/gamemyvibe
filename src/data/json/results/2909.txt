The Legend of Zelda: A Link Between Worlds is the first Zelda game for the Nintendo 3DS. It is an indirect sequel to A Link to the Past, featuring the same Hyrule but new characters and gameplay. Many areas are accessible from the start, dungeons can be visited in any order, and puzzles appear in both dungeons and the open world, with the 3DS touch screen showing the world, dungeon, or map.
